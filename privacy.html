Welcome to SoundMatch. Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information when you use the SoundMatch mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

We do not sell your personal data — ever.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use SoundMatch:

  • Profile information — display name, profile avatar, and profile banner image that you choose to provide.
  • Music activity — tracks you like, artists you follow, and your listening history within the App.
  • Usage and activity data — engagement metrics such as daily streaks, points earned, and in-app actions used to power features and personalise your experience.
  • Device identifiers — unique identifiers associated with your device, collected automatically via Google Firebase for authentication and crash reporting purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and maintain your account and profile.
  • Provide core App features including music discovery, recommendations, and social interactions.

  • Improve the App through analytics and crash diagnostics.
